Regenerative Braking Programme

KONE's EcoDisc and MonoSpace 700 units are equipped with regenerative drives that capture kinetic energy during descent and deceleration, feeding it back to the building's electrical grid. In 2025, this programme recovered 758 kWh — equivalent to powering a Finnish apartment for approximately 3 months.

The programme targets 15% recovery rate by 2027 through drive firmware upgrades and installation of high-capacity return units in our highest-traffic buildings.

Data Governance & Privacy

The KONE Energy Analytics platform processes building-level operational data only. No personally identifiable passenger information is collected or retained at any point in the data pipeline.

All energy readings, trip counts, and load metrics are stored in encrypted local databases and are subject to a 24-month retention policy, after which data is anonymised and aggregated for longitudinal benchmarking.

Third-party access to operational data is governed by data processing agreements compliant with GDPR Article 28, and all cloud integrations are subject to annual security reviews.
